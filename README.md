Template Haven is built for a few groups. 
-	Students look for planners, calendars, and resume templates to keep their lives organized and get a head start on their careers. 
-	Young professionals are out to craft a sharp resume or build up their brand on social media, so clean templates and social kits
  matter to them. 
-	Creatives and entrepreneurs who just need affordable website templates and solid design assets so they can launch
  something without a huge budget or long delays.
Everyone who comes to Template Haven has a few things in common. They want stuff that looks good, doesn’t cost a fortune,
and makes their lives easier. They know their way around tech and usually juggle school, work, maybe a side hustle.
Fast, professional, ready-to-go solutions are the things that really speak to them.
